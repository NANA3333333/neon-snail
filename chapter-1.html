Chapter 1: The Rain Has Teeth

The rain in Sector 4 doesn't wash things clean. It just makes the grime slicker.

Inspector Mochi adjusted his monocle. The gear on his top hat whirred softly, syncing with the city's heartbeat. He shifted his weight, favoring his mechanical left hind leg.

"Three legs are enough," he muttered to the empty alley. "Stability is a state of mind."
